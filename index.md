## Overview

[csdata](https://niphr.github.io/csdata/) contains preformatted
structural data for Norway.

Datasets relating to population in municipalities, municipality/county
matching, and how different municipalities have merged/redistricted over
time from 2006 to 2024.

Read the introduction vignette
[here](https://niphr.github.io/csdata/articles/csdata.html) or run
[`help(package="csdata")`](https://niphr.github.io/csdata/reference).
