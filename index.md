What’s inside

01

### Location hierarchy

Codes and display names for every Norwegian geographic unit — ward,
municipality, county, region, and nation — with functions to map between
levels.

02

### Population by age

Annual population counts (1 January) broken down by location and age
category, from national totals down to individual municipalities.

03

### Redistricting weights

Proportional weights that convert data recorded under old municipal
borders to the 2020 or 2024 boundaries, covering mergers and splits from
2006 onward.

## Overview

[csdata](https://niphr.github.io/csdata/) contains preformatted
structural data for Norway.

Datasets relating to population in municipalities, municipality/county
matching, and how different municipalities have merged/redistricted over
time from 2006 to 2024.

Read the introduction vignette
[here](https://niphr.github.io/csdata/articles/csdata.html) or run
[`help(package="csdata")`](https://niphr.github.io/csdata/reference).
