What’s inside

01

### Location hierarchy

Codes and display names for every Norwegian geographic unit — ward,
municipality, county, region, and nation — with functions to map between
levels.

02

### Population by age

Annual population counts (1 January) broken down by location and age
category, from national totals down to individual municipalities.

03

### Redistricting weights

Proportional weights that convert data recorded under old municipal
borders to the 2020 or 2024 boundaries, covering mergers and splits from
2006 onward.
